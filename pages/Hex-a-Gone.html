Hex-a-Gone

Description

It's a level with disappearing tiles.

Strategy

You run around until you are out of tiles. When you no longer have any tiles to run to, you need to jump to tiles further away. To do this, press the jump button. When tiles are especially far away, also press the dive button. Keep repeating this until all your opponents have fallen into the slime.

Congratulations, you now know everything you need to win a crown at Hex-a-Gone.
